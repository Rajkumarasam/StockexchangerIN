Technical Indicators for Algorithmic Success

Our platform harnesses the power of advanced technical indicators to help you make informed trading decisions based on market data and patterns.

Moving Averages

Identify trends by smoothing out price data over specific time periods, with support for SMA, EMA, WMA, and VWMA.

Relative Strength Index (RSI)

Measure momentum and identify overbought or oversold conditions with this powerful oscillator.

MACD

Use Moving Average Convergence Divergence to spot changes in strength, direction, momentum, and duration of trends.

Bollinger Bands

Visualize volatility and potential price breakouts with dynamic bands that expand and contract with market conditions.

Stochastic Oscillator

Compare closing price to price range over time to generate momentum-based signals for potential trend reversals.

Fibonacci Retracement

Identify potential support and resistance levels based on the golden ratio for precise entry and exit points.
